Common Queries

Get Answers to Common Queries. Your Questions, Addressed Simply.

Can you work with clients remotely?

Yes, I can work with clients remotely. I’m comfortable communicating through online platforms, managing tasks virtually, and collaborating effectively using tools like Google Meet, Zoom, Slack, and email to ensure smooth and efficient project delivery.

How long does it typically take to complete a project?

The timeline usually depends on the scope and complexity of the project. For example: - Small projects or landing pages typically take about 1–2 weeks. - Medium-sized websites (with multiple pages and features) may take 3–5 weeks. - Larger or custom projects with advanced functionality can take 6 weeks or more. I always provide a clear timeline after discussing the project requirements in detail to ensure transparency and on-time delivery.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes, I do offer basic website maintenance. This includes tasks like updating content, fixing minor bugs, ensuring everything runs smoothly, and making small design or functionality improvements when needed.

Can you optimize my website?

Yes, I can perform basic website optimization — such as improving loading speed, optimizing images, cleaning up code, and ensuring good responsiveness across devices. For more advanced SEO or performance optimization, I can research and apply suitable improvements as needed.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, absolutely! I always ensure that every website I create is fully responsive and mobile-friendly, meaning it will look great and function smoothly on all devices — whether it’s a phone, tablet, or desktop.

Can you integrate third-party tools into my website?

Yes, I can integrate some commonly used third-party tools into websites — like contact forms, analytics, payment gateways, or social media plugins. If a specific or complex integration is needed, I usually research and implement it carefully to make sure it works properly with the site.

How do you handle website revisions?

I usually share a preview of the website during development so you can review the progress. Once the initial version is ready, you can share your feedback or request changes. I make revisions based on that feedback to ensure the final result matches your expectations — usually within a few revision rounds, depending on the project scope.

What are your payment terms?

I usually take payments in two phases — an initial advance before starting the project and the remaining amount after completion and approval of the final website. Please note that if the project requirements or terms change frequently during development, the final payment amount may increase significantly, depending on the extra work or time required.
